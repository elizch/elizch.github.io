Wellesley Asks Nico

This website is a Q&A-based social platform created for Wellesley students to ask other students questions - either big or small - about academics, campus life, or any other part of the Wellesley experience. This platform also incorporates elements of human-AI collaboration, by also allowing users to receive answers from an AI bot.

FULL-STACK WEB DEVELOPMENT | HCI LAB

Six Hats Thinking Engine

The Six Hats Thinking Engine is a GPT-4 app for collaborative brainstorming. Following the model of the "Six Thinking Hats", this web app takes a problem statement and idea as input from the user and returns responses from six different brainstorming perspective agents.

APP DESIGN | CS220 @ WELLESLEY

The Good Place

The Good Place is an app focused on assisting Wellesley students in accessing mental health resources, understanding their emotions, and ultimately practicing effective self-care. This app is designed to provide resources (particularly those specific to Wellesley College) and give users a space to record and vent their emotions in a fun and non-intimidating way.

APP DESIGN | WHACK 2022

Safe Ventures

Traveling to new countries can be an exhilarating experience, but safety concerns are equally important to address. Safe Ventures is an app designed to allow users to share their travel stories and safety tips with a community of travelers.
